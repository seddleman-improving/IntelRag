# Past Engagement: Staff Augmentation for Scaling Retail E-Commerce Team

**Domain:** firm-proposal
**Client:** Southeast Home Furnishings Retailer (confidential — referenced anonymously)
**Industry:** Retail
**Service Type:** Staff augmentation
**Duration:** 14 months (ongoing at time of reference)
**Started:** Q1 2024
**Status:** Active engagement

## Engagement Summary

Our firm placed 4 engineers with a regional home furnishings retailer ($540M revenue) that had grown e-commerce from 8% to 19% of revenue in 2 years and could not hire fast enough to execute their digital roadmap. We provided immediate capacity while they built their internal team.

## Problem Statement

The client had 11 software engineers trying to support a Shopify Plus storefront, a Manhattan Associates OMS, a React Native loyalty app, and a Snowflake data platform simultaneously. Engineering velocity had dropped to releasing once every 6 weeks due to context switching and system instability. The OMS integration was causing overselling incidents averaging 3 times per week, directly impacting customer experience.

## What We Provided

1. **2 senior full-stack engineers** (TypeScript/React/Node) — embedded in the storefront team; within 90 days increased release cadence to weekly
2. **1 OMS integration specialist** — diagnosed and resolved the overselling root cause (race condition in inventory reservation logic); implemented event-driven inventory sync using AWS EventBridge
3. **1 React Native engineer** — rebuilt the loyalty app performance-critical screens; App Store rating improved from 2.4 to 4.1 stars within 4 months of fixes shipping

## Outcomes

- Overselling incidents reduced from 3/week to 0 in the 60 days following OMS fix
- Release cadence: 6 weeks → weekly releases within 90 days
- Loyalty app App Store rating: 2.4 → 4.1 stars
- Client hired 3 of 4 engineers as permanent employees at engagement end (highest form of success validation)

## Technologies Used

Shopify Plus, React, TypeScript, Node.js, Manhattan Associates OMS, React Native, AWS EventBridge, Snowflake

## Pain Points We Solved

- E-commerce team overwhelmed by competing initiatives
- OMS integration causing customer-facing overselling incidents
- Mobile app rated below 3 stars — executive priority to fix
- Release velocity too slow to execute digital roadmap

## Why This Engagement Worked

Staff augmentation was the right model because the client's problem was capacity, not capability. Their engineering leads knew what to build — they just didn't have enough hands. Our engineers integrated into existing teams (not a separate "consulting workstream") which accelerated onboarding and ensured the work was owned and understood by permanent staff from day one.
